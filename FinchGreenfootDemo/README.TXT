This scenario is a demo scenario for the finch, showing off its abilities.
It contains the following features:

- A thermometer, on the left that shows the current temperature.
- Arrows that show the direction of movement of the finch (use the arrow keys to control the movement).
- Light control, drag these sliders to change the proportion of red, green and blue in the finch's front LED.
- Acceleration display, this shows the X, Y and Z values of the finch's accelerometer.
- Obstacle display, these are the two green blocks that turn red when the finch detects an obstacle on its left or right side respectively.
- Light meter, this is displayed under the two green blocks and turns blacker the darker it is and whiter the lighter it is.
- Buzzer, click this to cause the finch to buzz.
